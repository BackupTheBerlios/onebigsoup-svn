
=== What is Local Names? ===

Local names is a way to bind ''names'' to ''URLs.''

Local Names is similar to DNS, but with two major differences:

* It's based on ''URLs,'' not IP addresses.
* Bindings are ''local,'' rather than ''global,'' in scope.

That is, two namespaces may bind "fred" in different ways.
